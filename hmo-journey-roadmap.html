Refinance

2-4 weeks
▼

The most powerful moment in the BRRR strategy. Your 3-bed purchase has become a fully licensed 6-8 bed HMO. The commercial valuation reflects this transformation — allowing you to pull your capital back out and repeat the process.

Mortgage broker instructed — specialist HMO refinance products identified
Refinance pack prepared — all certificates, licence, tenancy agreements, rent schedule
Valuation booked — commercial basis confirmed with broker
Valuation reviewed — reflects income-generating potential of the property
Refinance mortgage offer issued
Legal work completed
Capital released — amount pulled back out calculated
Cash-on-cash return calculated — how much of my money is still in the deal?
Portfolio tracker updated — total portfolio value and monthly income

★ Commercial Valuation Note

A fully converted 6-8 bed HMO with en-suites, fire doors, interlinked alarms and an HMO licence is valued on its income-generating potential — not just bricks and mortar. This is what makes the BRRR strategy so powerful. The gap between your all-in cost and the commercial valuation is where your wealth is created.

⚠ Watch Out

Refinancing before the property is fully let. Most lenders require full occupancy and at least one month of rental income evidence before lending. Make sure all rooms are let before instructing your broker.

Refinance complete, capital returned to your account. Go back to Stage 1.
