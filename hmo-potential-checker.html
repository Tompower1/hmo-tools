Commercial valuation is what makes BRRR work at scale. When a property is so far removed from a standard home — 6+ ensuite rooms, fire doors, interlinked alarms throughout — a lender will instruct a valuer to assess it on rental income rather than comparable house prices. This typically results in a significantly higher valuation, allowing you to pull more capital out at refinance.
A 3-bed converted to 6-7 ensuite rooms achieves full commercial valuation. A 3-bed converted to 5 beds without ensuites may only achieve bricks and mortar — the BRRR numbers rarely stack up in this scenario.
