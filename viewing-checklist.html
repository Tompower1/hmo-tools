Before You Go In

On arrival — before entering the property
▼
Walk the street both directions — what is it like? What are neighbouring properties like?
Check the exterior — roof condition, guttering, brickwork, windows, any visible damp or damage
Assess the garden from the street if visible — how deep does it look?
Note parking — on street, off street, permit zone?
Photograph the exterior and street before entering
💬

The Agent Conversation

Ask these questions before starting the viewing
▼

About the Vendor

Why is the vendor selling?
How long has it been on the market?
Have there been any offers? Why did they fall through?
Is the vendor in a chain?
How quickly are they looking to move?

About the Property

Has any planning permission been applied for previously?
Are there any known issues with the property?
What is included in the sale?
Is it currently tenanted? If so what are the tenancy terms?

⚠ What NOT to Say

  • Do not mention HMO conversion
  • Do not mention planning for additional rooms
  • Do not mention your intentions for the property
  • You are a property investor looking for a 3-bed. That is all.

Complete within 24 hours of getting home

Note your gut feeling — does this property excite you?
Compare against other properties on your shortlist
Make a clear decision — pursue or move on

The Decision Framework

Force a clear decision — no grey areas
▼

✓ Pursue this deal if:

  • The Potential Checker scored green
  • The numbers stack up on the Calculator Pro
  • No major structural red flags identified
  • Your gut says yes and the data backs it up

✗ Do not pursue if:

  • The Potential Checker scored red
  • The numbers do not work even at optimistic room rents
  • Major structural issues identified — subsidence, serious damp, roof problems
  • The garden does not have enough depth for an extension to reach 6+ rooms
  • The loft has insufficient head height and no extension potential — meaning you cannot get to 6+ rooms

⚠ Go back for a second viewing if:

  • The Potential Checker scored amber
  • You could not access the loft
  • You need to measure something you missed
  • You want to bring someone else — architect, builder, trusted investor
🚀

If You Are Pursuing the Deal

Next steps to secure the property
▼
Instruct a solicitor — get them ready to move when an offer is accepted
Speak to your mortgage broker — confirm finance is in place
Research comparable sold prices to inform your offer
Decide on your opening offer — what is the maximum you will pay and still make it work?
Make your offer
🔄

If No Qualifying Deal Found

The pipeline does not stop
▼
Go back to Day 3 of the Action Plan — follow up with all agents
Expand your search — adjacent postcodes, slightly larger radius
Re-run the Scorecard on a new target area if needed
The pipeline continues until you find the right deal

The Golden Rule

Never leave a viewing in a grey area. Force yourself to make a clear decision. Either you are pursuing this deal or you are not. Indecision costs more time than a wrong decision ever will.
